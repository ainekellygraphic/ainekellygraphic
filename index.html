Serpent and Queen

Dublin's disgusting tale of Romeo and Juliet

About

So what the hell is this all about, eh?

The serpent (Romeo), and queen (Juliet), are the stars of this tragic love story. It will be performed by a host of Ireland’s hottest upcoming actors and drag queens.

It is an inclusive event for people from all walks of like, who may be a bit different, and want to find like-minded folk. It is a space to feel truly yourself, and get a bit weird.

House Rules

This musical performance is a mixture of drag queens and dark comedy to convey this serious story of teenage suicide.
